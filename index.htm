Amen Manufacturing
Private Ltd. Company

The Company was established in the Oromia Region of Ethiopia based on Ethiopian commercial laws, and its headquarters is located in Addis Ababa. The Company was newly established in 2019 with the major business aim of growing, processing, and exporting coffee products. Hence, Amen Coffee Export Business organized and launched its business by cleaning and exporting coffee beans. Within its short period, since its establishment, the company has completed construction of coffee bean clearing and storage premises on 30,000 m2 of land that waiting for an Ar-rival coffee processing machine from abroad.




    We provide a wide range of tastes and flavors from all over Ethiopia. As a result, we get our coffee from these seven distinctively different regions of the nation. - Sidama, Guji, Yirgacheffe, Limmu, Harar, Jimma and Nekemte.
